		\item $(f \circ g)(x) = f(4x - k) = 6(4x - k) + k = 24x - 5k$
		\item $(g \circ f)(x) = g(6x + k) = 4(6x + k) - k = 24x + 3k$
		\item $(f - g)(k) = f(k) - g(k) = (6k + k) - (4k - k) = 7k - 3k = 4k$
	\end{itemize}
	Reemplazamos en la ecuaciΟ≥n original:
	\begin{align*}
		(24x - 5k) + 4 &= (24x + 3k) - 4k \\
		24x - 5k + 4 &= 24x - k \\

	\end{align*}
